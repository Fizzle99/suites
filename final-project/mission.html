Receptions

For temple missionaries, the following long-term suites are available:

Small

The small suite is ideal for temple missionaries on a budget. The following furnishings come standard:
  • One bed or two bed options, queen sized or twin!
  • Utilities including WiFi
  • Kitchenette and Laundery access

Medium

The medium suite has access to all amenities and options of the small suite. The following furnishings come with a medium size suite:
  • Full Kitchen
  • Appliances including dishwasher, and clothes washer/dryer.

Large

The large suite is ideal for groups of temple missionaries travelling together.
  • All furnishings from medium and small suites
  • Multiple bedrooms, 2-3, each with single or double bed options.

Custom

The custom suite option is ideal for temple missionaries with need to access more refined amenities or have accomadations for disability. Inquire on reservation in the comments section for information.
