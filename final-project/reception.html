Wedding Receptions at Temple Inns & Suites

A temple wedding is a beautiful, essential ordinance in the lives of the Latter Day Saints. Our hotels aim to offer a reception fitting the joy and festivity of such an event.

Catering

In-house catering is affordable and delicious, prepared by our many chefs with a sprawling menu of delicacies to enjoy. We provide meals at the following timeslots:

Day

-8:00 AM to 4:00 PM

Evening

-5:00 PM to 11:00 PM

If our menu isn't to your taste, there are several restaurants near each hotel site from which you can order catering. However, hosting outside services will have a small fee of $25.00.

Setup and Staffing

Our hosts and hostesses are friendly, efficient, and spirited staff to wait your reception. Our team will make sure that everything runs smoothely, and handle the after-reception cleanup promptly. Everyone has something special to do on the day of a wedding, so let our people make things easier.

Setup and Takedown of receptions are billed at $20.00 per staff member per hour, or a team of 6 for $100.00 an hour. Staffing and waiting the reception will be billed at $250.00 for 2 hours, with an extra $50.00 per hour after.
